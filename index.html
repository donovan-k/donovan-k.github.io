About Me

Hi, I'm Donovan! I'm a Musician/Skater with a passion for creating rock, hyperwave, house, and other kinds of music. I also enjoy skating and designing decks. This website is dedicated to showcasing my work and sharing my experiences in the vibrant world of sound and skate.
